OCC Internationalization Ambassadors Organization

The OCC Internationalization Ambassadors Organization (OCC-IAO) is established to serve as a vital extension of the Office for Internationalization, empowering students to actively participate in and contribute to the college's internationalization initiatives.

The OCC-IAO, which is based on the ideas of internationalization and global citizenship, aims to produce globally capable student leaders who can represent the college in a variety of international engagements while fostering a friendly and inclusive environment for foreign partners, visitors, and guests.
